### Externalities

&emsp;&emsp;When an activity affects a bystander.

## Negative Externality	

- Qm is too high
- Pollution
- Loud Noise
- Cell Phone Driving
- Second-hand Smoking

## Positive Externality

- Qm is too low
- Wearing a mask
- Education
- Fire Extinguisher
- Flu Shot
